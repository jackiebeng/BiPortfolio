Ikigai Diagram

To start, I feel like these are meant to change over time. But identifying something is better than nothing. As of April 2023, here is what I have for my Ikigai diagram.

For what I love, I love music, making content, the outdoors, tech, money, and making meaningful connections. For what I am good at, I would say I'm good at musical instruments, photography, time management, and being organized, whether that is digitally or in real life. This also leads to what I can get paid to do: tech-related jobs, leadership and management roles, creating media, designing, and, of course, business related. Last but not least, What does the world need? I think the world needs better access/help with mental health, the environment, and of course, equality.

What do all of these have in common? They all have arts, tech, and business-related topics. This also made me question what I want to do moving forward. These allowed me to see what interests me and what I can gear my work and passion toward while also having topics in the world that can be improved, such as mental health. With this information, I can also better understand how I can combine "My strengths, Skills, and Passion to positively impact those around me."
